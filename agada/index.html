Aggada vs. Halacha

The Talmud (ש"ס) can be broken down into two main categories: The definitions, sources and explanations of all Jewish law know as Halacha (הלכה). And the principles of Jewish faith, philosophy, ethics and stories known as Agada (אגדה).
